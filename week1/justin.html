Biography

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist who was discovered by talent manager Scooter Braun through his YouTube videos in 2007. Bieber's debut album, "My World," released in 2009, catapulted him to international stardom. Known for hits like "Baby," "Sorry," and "Love Yourself," Justin has become one of the best-selling music artists in the world. His music blends pop, R&B, and dance influences, making him a global icon and a beloved artist among fans.
